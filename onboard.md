Screen 1 — Welcome shows four feature cards (DeFi, DWT rewards, AI agent, market data) so users understand what they're getting before they commit. Two clear buttons: Create or Import.
Screen 2 — Security briefing (new) — before creating a password, every user must read and agree to 4 key security facts: you own your keys, seed phrase = master key, no recovery without it, we can't freeze funds. A checkbox forces them to acknowledge this. This dramatically reduces support requests and sets expectations correctly.
Screen 3a — Create shows a real-time password strength meter with 4 checks (length, uppercase, number, symbol) and a color-coded bar. The button stays disabled until the password is valid and confirmed.
Screen 3b — Import accepts 12 or 24 word phrases with a live word count indicator.

Screen 4 — Backup shows the seed phrase blurred with a "tap to reveal" overlay — privacy protection for public spaces. Two mandatory checkboxes before continuing.

Screen 5 — Verify (improved) instead of asking users to retype all 12 words, it picks 3 random words and asks only those. Much better UX while still confirming they saved it. Each word gets a live ✓ or ✗ as they type.

Screen 6 — Complete shows a success animation with a checklist of what was set up, a referral program teaser, and auto-redirects to the wallet after 1.8 seconds.
